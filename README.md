## experiments

- [tetris](tetris/index.html) - a remake of the iconic tile-matching game originally designed by  in 1985.
- [game of life](game-of-life/index.html) - conway's game of life, a zero-player cellular automaton devised by john conway in 1970.
- [memento mori](memento-mori/index.html) - memento mori, latin for "remember that you will die", visualized.
- [izvir](izvir/index.html) - a visual exploration of pixelated geometric shapes.
- [grid lines](grid-lines/index.html) - a visual exploration of a random walk.
- [unshredder](unshredder/index.html) - inspired by the instagram engineering challenge, reconstruction of a shredded image by pixel-matching adjacent strip edges.
