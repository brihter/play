experiments

  • tetris - a remake of the iconic tile-matching game originally designed by in 1985.
  • game of life - conway's game of life, a zero-player cellular automaton devised by john conway in 1970.
  • memento mori - memento mori, latin for "remember that you will die", visualized.
  • izvir - a visual exploration of pixelated geometric shapes.
  • grid lines - a visual exploration of a random walk.
  • unshredder - inspired by the instagram engineering challenge, reconstruction of a shredded image by pixel-matching adjacent strip edges.
